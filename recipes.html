Recipes 

Korean beef lettuce cups 

These fresh and spicy lettuce cups are a perfect light meal. This Korean dish is nutritious and super healthy. The crispy lettuce, crunchy carrots and roasted sesame seeds pair perfectly with our 100% grass-fed beef. Print recipe here....

Hampton Gay bolognese 

This is one of our family favourites. Takes a couple of hours but it is totally worth it. The milk makes the sauce creamy and takes the acidity away form the tomatoes. Serve topped with parmesan and finely chopped basil. Print recipe here....

Beef tacos  

A deliciously easy mid-week family favourite. Pair our 100% pasture fed, organic beef with fresh lime creme fraiche, guacamole and homemade salsa. This recipe couldn't be more simple.   Print recipe here....

Quick bolognese 

A quick and easy mid-week meal made with Real Organic Pasta Sauces from our farm shop. Pair our 100% pasture fed, organic beef with the spicy organic Arrabbiata sauce or for a more traditional bolognese, the Sicilian Tomato and Olive sauce. Print recipe here....

Marinated roast beef 

Perfect for oyster blade, silver side or topside cuts. For maximum flavour marinade your joint for 2-3 days. Serve with mash, peas and the carrots roasted underneath the beef. Print recipe here....

Beef shin ragu 

An Italian inspired pasta dish thats perfect for slow-cooking. Leave it in the oven in the morning and 4 hours later you have beautifully tender beef shin. The perfect accompaniment to organic spelt rigatoni. Print recipe here....
